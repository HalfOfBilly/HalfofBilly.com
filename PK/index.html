EPILEPSY WARNING: This archive contains flashing and rapid-animated imagery.

From the creators of The Town With No Name comes Psycho Killer (PK), a "graphic action-adventure game" involving protagonist John Schultz (listed in the credits but otherwise unnamed) hunting down a killer to save himself and Sarah Collins, who he witnessed being kidnapped by said murderer.

This project aims to extract and archive all of PK's assets into more common file types so they may be used by anyone for anything (videos, mods, etc).

If I have missed any important assets, or you have information about extracting certain file types, or have any other questions, please Send me an email or Leave a comment in my Guestbook.
